Thumbelina


Once upon a time, nestled within a fragrant flower, a tiny girl named Thumbelina came into the world. The old woman who discovered her, charmed by her delicate beauty, adopted Thumbelina as her own. However, fate had other plans for the little girl.



One night, while the old woman slept, a mischievous toad crept into their home and carried Thumbelina away, intending her to marry his son. Yet, Thumbelina's spirit was as strong as her stature was small. Determined to find her own path, she escaped into the wide world beyond.


Her journey was fraught with danger and wonder. Along the way, she encountered helpful creatures like a motherly field mouse and a caring swallow. Despite the challenges, Thumbelina never lost hope, her heart warmed by the kindness of those she met.
